    What to Calculate?

    FitCalc gives you ability to calculate various aspects of your fitness such as body mass index(BMI), personal caloric requirement and calories burnt while doing different activities.

    BMI

    Calculate your body mass index(BMI) which will help your determine which weight categary you fall in.

    Calorie requirement

    Calculate your personal daily caloric requirement and find out how many calories you should consume daily to lose/gain weight.

    Calories Burnt

    Calculate the amount of calories burnt by your body while doing different physical activities such as running, skipping and cycling.
